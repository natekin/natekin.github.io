F.A.Q.

Is attendance free?

Yes, everything is free and community-oriented. All materials will also be publicly available. All you have to do is register, get an invite and come visit us.

Where will Data Fest take place?

Data Fest is held at Odessa, Ukraine, at STEP Computer Academy on Sadavaya st. 3. For official invites to visit Ukraine please contact Fest organizers (below).

What should I do to participate?

You fill out the registration form. Then we rpocess requests and send out invites. With the invite on hand all you have to do is just come to the conference and have fun.

What if I am from afar?

Don't worry, we are very open to travellers and grant them invites. If you're from Russia, you might need an invite from organizers (to ensure border passage, which is much better now).

Will there be a livestream?

Probably not, but we are working on it. We will try our best and videos will likely be recorded, but livestream is harder.
